A linux desktop version of Google Authenticator in your tray

Tue 29 October 2013

A while ago, I activated the 2-step verification with my google account. It sure enhances the security, but I was always scared of what would happen if I loose my phone and my backup codes at the same time. Not any more!

I discovered that you could actually generate the same codes with oathtool, from the oath-toolkit project.
